Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: To save our work in case it gets lost.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: It can be applied to finance. It can be used to find trends in stock market data or predict future prices.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging will combine two branches with a new merge commit and preserve the history and rebase will rewrite the history and apply changes of a branch on top of another branch.
